# FRTB

The Fundamental Review of the Trading Book (FRTB) is a regulatory framework designed to improve how trading book risk is measured and capitalised.

## What this section covers
- Scope and desk classification under FRTB
- Risk sensitivities and capital calculation concepts
- Governance and implementation considerations
- Relationship to broader market risk management

## Why it matters
FRTB affects the way trading activities are measured and governed. Clear documentation supports implementation, reporting, and control design.
