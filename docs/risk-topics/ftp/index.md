# FTP

Funding transfer pricing (FTP) allocates funding costs and benefits across business units to support internal pricing, performance measurement, and decision-making.

## What this section covers
- The purpose of FTP in a banking context
- Methodological approaches and pricing conventions
- Role in profitability analysis and management reporting
- Common implementation considerations

## Why it matters
FTP provides a structured way to reflect funding economics in product pricing and business performance. It is especially relevant for ALM and treasury-related decision support.
