# Balance Sheet Management

Balance sheet management focuses on how a bank shapes and controls the composition of assets and liabilities to meet strategic, financial, and risk objectives.

## What this section covers
- Asset-liability management concepts
- Hedging and structural balance sheet actions
- Key constraints and strategic trade-offs
- Reporting and governance practices

## Why it matters
Balance sheet management links business strategy, funding, liquidity, and risk appetite. Strong documentation helps teams align operational decisions with the wider risk framework.
