# Liquidity Risk

Liquidity risk relates to the ability to meet funding needs, honour obligations, and withstand stress conditions without unacceptable losses or disruptions.

## What this section covers
- Liquidity metrics and monitoring frameworks
- Funding and market liquidity concepts
- Stress testing and contingency planning
- Governance and reporting practices

## Why it matters
Liquidity management is essential for continuity, resilience, and regulatory compliance. This documentation will help connect measurement with practical management actions.
